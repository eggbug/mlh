Symptoms of Coronavirus

If you have any symptoms then contact your doctor immediately.

High Fever

If you have a high fever monitor your fever and call the doctor once you found out.

Cough

Coughing is another symptom. If you cough it is a symptom, but anyone can have the cough. It could be a cold not covid.

Sore Troath

Having a sore troath is a big symptom once you get it then you call your doctor immediately.

Headache

If you have a headache it is a possibility you have covid-19 but if you have it then monitor it for a few days before calling your doctor.

Stay at home and call your doctor: If you have any symptoms of Covid-19.
